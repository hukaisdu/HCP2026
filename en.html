About HCP

Introduction

The Workshop on Hard Computational Problems: Theories, Algorithms and Applications (HCP) promotes in-depth exchange among researchers and industry practitioners working on hard computational problems.

The workshop focuses on problem representations, theoretical algorithms, practical algorithms, and real-world applications, including logical constraints, mathematical programming, graph theory, combinatorial optimization, constraint solving, SAT/SMT, knowledge compilation, and learning-assisted optimization.

Previous HCP workshops have been held in Changchun, Dalian, Yinchuan, Duyun, Kunming, Yantai, and Shanghai. HCP 2026 will be hosted at Shandong University.
